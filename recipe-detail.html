Nano's Golden Pancake Stack

Ingredients

  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in buttermilk and eggs until smooth.
  3. Cook on a buttered skillet over medium heat until golden.
  4. Top with honey and blueberries and serve immediately.
